    The jaguar is the largest cat in South America. It lives in rainforests such as the Amazon rainforests. These creatures use their powerful leaping abilities, claws, and and jaws to attack its prey.

    Snakes are very interesting reptiles for many reasons. Its way of travel is different than other kinds of animals. It slithers across the ground and picks up the scent of its prey by using its tongue. These creatures also have a venomous bite to attack its foes.

  5. Piranhas

    Piranhas live in freshwaters located in South America. They are powerful fish because of their bites. Multiple bites from these creatures can easily tear flesh. When these creatures are in numbers, they can take on even some of the larger creatures that live underwater
